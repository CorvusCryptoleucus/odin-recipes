About PalRecipes

Welcome to PalRecipes, your destination for vegan Palestinian recipes! Originally created as a portfolio project for The Odin Project, this website continues to evolve as a showcase of my journey in web development. Initially tasked with creating a recipe website featuring three recipes, it has since grown to focus on Palestinian cuisine out of solidarity with the people of Gaza and the West Bank.

As a vegan and someone who is not Palestinian, I have chosen to feature exclusively vegan Palestinian recipes to promote Palestinian culture and cuisine in a respectful manner. It's important to note that while I am passionate about learning and promoting Palestinian culture through food, I am not an expert in Palestinian cuisine.

This project aims to contribute to cultural preservation and raise awareness against Palestinian erasure, all while offering delicious vegan options. While it is unlikely that I will continue to add new recipes to this website, your feedback and suggestions are always welcome. If you have a recipe you'd like to see included or any other feedback, feel free to let me know.

The photos used on this site, unless otherwise noted, are sourced from various public domain and Creative Commons sources around the internet. Sources are always documented in the code for transparency and credit.

The purpose of PalRecipes primarily serves as a portfolio piece, showcasing my skills in web development and my commitment to creating bilingual content. As I continue to learn Spanish, I believe in the importance of bilingual accessibility, and thus the entire website is available in Spanish as well. You can access the Spanish version of the site [here](#link).

Thank you for visiting PalRecipes. I hope you find these recipes not only enjoyable but also a meaningful way to explore Palestinian culture and support the vegan lifestyle.

Happy cooking!
