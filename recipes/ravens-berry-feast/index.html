Raven’s Berry Feast

Description

This vibrant mix of seasonal berries—blueberries, blackberries, and raspberries—is tossed with a sprinkle of wildflower seeds and a drizzle of agave nectar. It celebrates Odin’s ravens, who gather information and share wisdom.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup blackberries
  • 1 cup raspberries
  • 2 tablespoons wildflower seeds
  • 2 tablespoons agave nectar

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the berries.
  2. Drizzle agave nectar over the berries and toss gently.
  3. Sprinkle wildflower seeds on top and serve immediately.
