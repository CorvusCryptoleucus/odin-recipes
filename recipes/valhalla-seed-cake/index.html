Valhalla Seed Cake

Description

This delicious seed cake is made with a blend of sunflower, pumpkin, and flaxseeds, mixed with crushed oats, maple syrup, and coconut oil. It provides a high-energy treat for birds, celebrating the nurturing aspect of Valhalla.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1/2 cup flaxseeds
  • 1 cup crushed oats
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine all the ingredients and mix well.
  3. Scoop the mixture into small molds or shape into cakes.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden.
  5. Let cool before serving.
