Mimir's Nutty Snack

Description

This nutty snack combines various nuts—walnuts, almonds, and pecans—with dried fruits and a sprinkle of rosemary and thyme. It provides a nutritious boost for birds and reflects the wisdom associated with Mimir in Norse mythology.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup walnuts
  • 1/2 cup almonds
  • 1/2 cup pecans
  • 1/4 cup dried fruits (e.g., raisins, cranberries)
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine all the nuts and dried fruits.
  2. Add the rosemary and thyme, and mix well.
  3. Serve in small portions or use as a topping for other bird treats.
